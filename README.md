## Projects

### Bike Share Analysis

Analyzed bike-sharing rental data using Tableau and R to determine how temperature, seasonality, weather conditions, and calendar effects influence rider demand.

**Tools:** Tableau, R, Statistical Analysis, Data Visualization

**Skills Demonstrated:** Dashboard Development, Correlation Analysis, Trend Modeling, Exploratory Data Analysis

### Sakila Movie Rental Business Analysis

Used SQL to analyze operational and financial data from the Sakila movie rental database, evaluating customer demographics, inventory value, store performance, and revenue generation.

**Tools:** SQL, CloudBeaver, Relational Databases

**Skills Demonstrated:** SQL Joins, Aggregate Functions, Revenue Analysis, Customer Analytics, Business Intelligence

---

### Supply Chain Shipment Logistics Analysis

Developed a Tableau dashboard to analyze global supply chain purchasing and shipment data, identifying trends in freight costs, shipment methods, purchasing activity, and product distribution.

**Tools:** Tableau, Data Visualization, Dashboard Development

**Skills Demonstrated:** Supply Chain Analytics, Logistics Analysis, Geographic Mapping, KPI Reporting, Data Storytelling
